It’s not waste until you waste it
–- E-waste recycling matters!

E-waste, or electronic waste, is one of the fastest-growing environmental challenges of our time. Discarded electronic devices, such as smartphones, laptops, and batteries, contain hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium, which can contaminate soil and water if not disposed of properly
